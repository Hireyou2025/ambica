Deep Sea Concept

The Story of Ambica Aquarium

Located in the heart of Srinagar, Kakinada (Second Floor, Above Domino's Pizza), Ambica Aquarium Restaurant introduces a revolutionary dining concept where culinary arts meet marine biology.

Our restaurant hosts giant, custom-engineered glass aquariums wrapping around the dining tables, displaying a vibrant array of tropical coral reefs, sharks, and schools of exotic fishes. The glowing neon illumination and ocean acoustics create an unmatched luxury sensory experience.

We are committed to using fresh catch-of-the-day seafood, hand-chosen local spices, and traditional recipes. Whether you're planning a romantic dinner, a family get-together, or a corporate event, our space delivers premium taste and memories that linger long after the night ends.

Luxury Ambiance

Dining Inside a Coral Reef

Ambica is designed with custom acrylic panels running floor-to-ceiling, containing over 50,000 liters of treated saltwater ecosystem.

As you dine, gaze directly into the vivid environment of Clownfish, Blue Tangs, stingrays, and miniature sharks swimming alongside coral structures. The restaurant interior operates on programmed LED light cycles that fade from bright aqua reef during lunch hours to a deep coral neon glow during dinner, creating a true deep-sea visual tour.
