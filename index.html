Our Legacy

Serving Happiness Since Inception

Located strategically along the busy Srikakulam - Ichchapuram Highway (Sunnadevi, Raghunadhapuram), Amaravati Family Restaurant has become a favorite rest stop for long-distance travelers and a beloved dining spot for local families.

We take pride in our rich culinary heritage. Our experienced chefs use hand-ground spices and fresh, locally sourced ingredients to create mouthwatering Biryanis and rich curries. We enforce strict hygiene standards across our kitchen and dining spaces to ensure a safe dining experience.

With clean environment, hospitable service, and 24/7 staff ready to serve you piping hot meals, we ensure your journey is filled with pleasant taste memories.
